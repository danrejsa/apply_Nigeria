About

Apply-Nigeria is an online web application designed to guide its users in the process of discovering and selecting suitable academic institutions that meets their individual requirements.

Together with our partners, we strive to provide quality and reliable information for our customers. Information found on this site has been vetted, verified, and is 100% accurate.

At Apply-Nigeria, we value leadership, integrity and service. It is our vision to be the hub of all school related information necessary for the school application process. Our mission statement is to remain market leaders that provide independent qualitative, reliable and accurate school related information.
