Ahmadu Ribadu College

Ahmadu Ribadu College is a privately own school established in the year 2000. It is an offshoot of Col. Isa Ahmed Memorial School. It started with 12 students and eight teachers. Ahmadu Ribadu College moved to its permanent site on 2/10/2003. The school is named after the Administrators father Late Ahmadu Ribadu a known educationist and a career diplomat. From 2003 to date the college has witnessed a geometrical growth both in infrastructure and in academic pursuits. Our first graduands performed very well indeed. The college recorded the best result in Adamawa State and rubbed shoulders with the best secondary school in the country.

Academic Program

Ahmadu Ribadu College is a fast growing institution with great hope for the future. The facilities it has provided for learning makes it one of the fast growing schools in Nigeria.

  • Hands on practical learning experience, using information technology with every student being technologically knowledgeable and possessing a Tablet computer as an academic tool.
  • Small class size which allows the teacher to pay attention to every child’s individual academic needs for effective learning
  • An enhanced academic program which supplements and supports the National curriculum with American standard texts, educational resources and style of delivery
