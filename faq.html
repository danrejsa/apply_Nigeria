What is our objective?

To create a centralized hub of secondary school information to guide the school selection and application process for your child.

How do we achieve our objective?

This application will enable users view the best performing schools in the country based on academic rankings, view basic individual school information, and apply to schools of interest.

Who can benefit from Apply - Nigeria?

Parents in Nigeria would benefit from this system, that is designed to provide a hub of country-wide school related information that is necessary when selecting the right academic environment for their children.

What is the scope of the project?

Apply - Nigeria covers all public and private secondary schools within Nigeria

What are the current challenges faced by Apply - Nigeria?

Most of the secondary schools in Nigeria are not connected online **Don't even have a website**.

Can I apply to any school of my choice on this platform?

No! Apply - Nigeria will only guide in the identification and selection process. In terms of application, we can give you the link to the school website (if it exist) to help you apply.

How do you obtain the result for each of the listed institution?

We have a memorandum of understanding with the examination body, thus, All results are obtained directly from the examination body.
