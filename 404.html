Our educational model is based on our mission statement: Partnership with parents to give an all-round education to each child based on the dignity of the human person, integrity, leadership qualities and academic excellence. Our mission is achieved through a strong collaboration between parents and our highly motivated members of staff. Our students are helped to achieve excellent academic performance, good character and strong moral principles through a methodology based on instilling virtues and values from a very early age. The tutorial system is the core channel through which we harmonise the education given at home and at school.

The Regent Secondary School

The Regent Secondary School offers an inclusive, academically challenging and selective, co-educational British-style Day and Boarding school education to some 350 local, national and international students from Years 7 to 11. The British National curriculum is adapted for years 7 and 8, with CIE Examinations in Year 9, and the prestigious IGCSE Curriculum and external examinations in Years 10 and 11. The Regent Secondary School is a new, purpose-built campus with state-of-the-art ICT, library and sporting facilities. Our mission is to develop responsible global citizens, and to cultivate leadership through academic excellence. For more than 14 years, The Regent Family of Schools has worked hard to establish itself as a well-known, first-rate educational choice for students and parents in Abuja and beyond. Teachers, board members, non-academic staff and friends of the school work together in a collegial manner to achieve excellence, and everything we do is student-centered. Although academic achievement is our priority, we also place emphasis on a comprehensive Co-curricular Activities Program where there is something to offer every student, whether it is academically related, sports or other activities of interest.
