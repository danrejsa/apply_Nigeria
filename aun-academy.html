AUN Academy

AUN Academy seeks to create a challenging learning environment that encourages high expectations for success through developing appropriate instruction that allows for individual differences and learning styles. Our school promotes a safe, orderly, caring, and supportive environment. Each student's self-esteem is fostered by positive relationships with students and staff members. We strive to have parents, teachers, and community members actively involved in our students' learning. Academic excellence is highly encouraged. For this reason, we have highly qualified and professional teachers with local and international experience on our team. We promote self-discipline as the key to achieving success and progress. We also foster culture of respect and diversity, being an international institution accommodating people from different backgrounds. AUN Academy educates its students for success and to meet the changing needs of society.

Academic Program

The American Track of the AUN Academy provides a curriculum that fully meets the Nigerian national curriculum standard and also offer a broader spectrum of knowledge from international resources and standards. The track has the following features;

  • The program prepares students towards writing the BECE (Junior Certificate), NECO, WASSCE and the Scholastic Attitude Tests (SAT I & SAT II)
  • Hands on practical learning experience, using information technology with every student being technologically knowledgeable and possessing a Tablet computer as an academic tool.
  • Opportunities for community service and other volunteer work to help build a sense of civic responsibility in the student and provide outlets for them to make impacts on the community with their learning.
  • An environment that encourages critical thinking and practical application of the concepts taught in class through life skill integration, laboratory and field trips every term to enhance learning process.
