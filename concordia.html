Concordia College

Concordia College was established primarily to fill a void in this part of the country and even beyond for education. It is a mixed boarding school with excellent facilities for learning. The staffs of the school are committed to its continuing development and improvement and to the enjoyment and success of every individual student. Over the years, we have been able to build a reputation as a lively, caring school and an exciting place for students to learn. Much commendation have been extended to us over the years btu of particular mention is the 2007 award by the National Association of Nigerian Students (NANS) for best secondary school in Nigeria. The percentage pass of our students with at least a credit in at least 5 subjects including English and Maths now stands at 100%.Students are encouraged and supported throughout their time at Concordia college to achieve in everything they do. The school has a happy and purposeful atmosphere. We believe that the learning environment we provide allows students to grow and develop acadamically, socially and personally into responsible, caring and confident young people.
